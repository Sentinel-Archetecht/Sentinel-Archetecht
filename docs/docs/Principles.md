# Core Principles

**Sentinel Security Protocol**

## Foundational Principles

### 1. True Digital Sovereignty
Users maintain full control over their data and identity.

### 2. Physical & Technical Isolation
Faraday-grade shielding and controlled environments.

### 3. Autonomous Verification
Mathematical and autonomous verification mechanisms.

### 4. Physical-Reality Anchoring
Grounding systems in verifiable physical reality.

### 5. Local-First & Edge Processing
Keep processing on-device when possible.

### 6. Recursive & Transparent Governance
Auditable governance systems.

### 7. Defense in Depth
Multiple independent protective layers.

### 8. Non-Proprietary Transparency
High-level concepts are shared publicly.
